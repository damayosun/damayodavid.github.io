  • in the country

    In the Philippines, the landscape of facial hair tends to favor clean-shaven looks, making the choice to grow a mustache a distinctive departure from the prevailing grooming norms. It's a less common style in this cultural context, adding an element of uniqueness to those who embrace it. By opting for a mustache in a society where smoother faces dominate, individuals carve out their own path in the realm of personal style, inviting curiosity and often sparking conversations about the unconventional choice.This cultural rarity not only sets one apart visually but also reflects a certain boldness in defying the prevailing grooming trends of the region.
